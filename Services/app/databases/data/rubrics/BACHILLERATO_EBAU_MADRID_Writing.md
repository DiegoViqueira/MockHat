# EBAU Writing Assessment RUBRIC - Madrid

This task consists of writing a simple text of various types (opinion essay, personal messages, descriptions, or narratives) with a length of 150 to 200 words. The student must demonstrate their ability to express themselves freely, interact, or mediate in writing in English. Reading comprehension is also necessary to understand the instructions and proposed topics. Two options will be offered, from which the student must choose one. The topics proposed may relate to the text or any other subject of personal relevance or general interest.

The following assessment scale will be used to grade this writing task:

**Score: from 0 to 3.** Each section will be scored between 0 and 0.5, depending on how well it aligns with the descriptor of “Excellent” (maximum score of 0.5) or “Poor” (minimum score of 0).
